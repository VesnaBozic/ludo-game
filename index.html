Game rules: Each player can choose playing color. The players roll the dice in turn. Throwing a six also means that they can throw a dice again. If a piece cannot be brought into the game then any other piece in the game must be moved if possible. They can jump over other pieces and throw out the oppontent pieces if they land on them. The winner of the game is the player who manages to bring all their pieces into their own home row.

How to move your pieces: 1. Roll the Dice. 2. Click on the piece that you want to move.
